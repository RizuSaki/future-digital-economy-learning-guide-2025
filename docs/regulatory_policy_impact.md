## EU AI Act Implementation

### Risk-Based Classification
1. **Unacceptable Risk**: Social scoring, biometric identification systems
2. **High Risk**: Safety-critical applications, biometric systems
3. **Limited Risk**: AI systems with specific transparency obligations
4. **Minimal Risk**: General AI applications with no specific risks

### Compliance Requirements
- **Risk Management**: Systematic risk assessment and mitigation
- **Data Governance**: High-quality datasets, bias prevention
- **Technical Documentation**: Comprehensive system documentation
- **Transparency**: Clear information about AI system capabilities
- **Human Oversight**: Human-in-the-loop for high-risk systems
- **Accuracy**: Robustness, accuracy, and cybersecurity

### Implementation Timeline
- **Prohibited Systems**: 6 months from entry into force
- **High-Risk Systems**: 24 months for implementation
- **General Purpose AI**: 12 months for compliance
- **Market Surveillance**: Ongoing monitoring and enforcement

## Data Protection & Privacy Regulation

### GDPR Implementation
- **Consent Management**: Clear, informed consent mechanisms
- **Data Subject Rights**: Right to access, correct, delete data
- **Privacy by Design**: Integration of privacy in system design
- **Data Protection Officer**: Mandatory appointment for certain organizations
- **Breach Notification**: Mandatory notification within 72 hours

### Global Privacy Frameworks
- **CCPA**: California Consumer Privacy Act implementation
- **PIPEDA**: Canadian Personal Information Protection Act
- **LGPD**: Brazil's General Data Protection Law
- **PDPA**: Singapore Personal Data Protection Act
- **China's Data Protection**: Cybersecurity Law and Data Security Law

### Cross-Border Data Transfers
- **Standard Contractual Clauses**: EU-approved transfer mechanisms
- **Adequacy Decisions**: Recognition of adequate protection levels
- **Binding Corporate Rules**: Internal data transfer policies
- **Certification Schemes**: Privacy certification for compliance
- **Derogations**: Specific situations for data transfers

## Learning Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (0-4 bulan)
1. **Regulatory Fundamentals**: Basic concepts and frameworks
2. **Technology Regulation**: How technology is regulated
3. **Privacy Law**: GDPR, CCPA, global privacy frameworks
4. **AI Ethics**: Ethical principles for AI development
5. **Compliance Basics**: Risk assessment, compliance management

### Phase 2: Specialization (4-8 bulan)
1. **AI Governance**: Detailed understanding of AI regulations
2. **Financial Regulation**: Fintech, cryptocurrency, banking regulation
3. **Data Protection**: Privacy engineering, compliance implementation
4. **International Law**: Cross-border regulatory coordination
5. **Regulatory Technology**: Compliance automation, RegTech

### Phase 3: Advanced Application (8-12 bulan)
1. **Policy Development**: Creating regulatory frameworks
2. **Industry Regulation**: Sector-specific regulatory requirements
3. **Regulatory Sandboxes**: Innovation promotion frameworks
4. **Compliance Strategy**: Enterprise compliance planning

### Phase 4: Expert Level (12+ bulan)

2. **International Coordination**: Global regulatory harmonization
