# Global Economics & Markets Learning Path

## Ringkasan Eksekutif

Ekonomi global mengalami transformasi mendasar di era digital 2025 dengan integrasi teknologi digital, blockchain, AI, dan sistem pembayaran baru. Pemahaman mendalam tentang ekonomi digital, tren pasar global, dan analisis ekonomi makro menjadi keterampilan penting untuk profesional ekonomi masa depan.

## Learning Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (0-6 bulan)
1. **Economic Fundamentals**: Microeconomics, macroeconomics basics
2. **Financial Markets**: Stock markets, bond markets, derivatives
3. **Global Economics**: International trade, development economics
4. **Statistics & Econometrics**: Statistical analysis, regression
5. **Data Analysis**: Excel, R, Python for economic analysis

### Phase 2: Digital Focus (6-12 bulan)
1. **Digital Economy Theory**: Platform economics, network effects
2. **Cryptocurrency Economics**: Digital currency analysis
3. **Fintech Analysis**: Financial technology impact
4. **AI Economics**: Economic impact of artificial intelligence
5. **Economic Modeling**: Advanced econometric models

### Phase 3: Advanced Application (12-18 bulan)
1. **Economic Forecasting**: Predictive modeling, scenario analysis
2. **Market Research**: Consumer behavior, market trends
3. **Policy Analysis**: Economic impact assessment
4. **International Markets**: Global economic analysis
5. **Risk Management**: Economic risk assessment, hedging

### Phase 4: Expert Level (18+ bulan)
1. **Economic Strategy**: Corporate economic strategy
2. **Investment Analysis**: Portfolio management, asset allocation
3. **Economic Consulting**: Advisory services for organizations
4. **Research & Development**: Economic research, publication
5. **Policy Development**: Economic policy design, implementation
