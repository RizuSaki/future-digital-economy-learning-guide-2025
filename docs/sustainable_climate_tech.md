# Sustainable & Climate Technology Learning Path

## Ringkasan Eksekutif

Teknologi berkelanjutan dan iklim mengalami akselerasi signifikan di 2025 dengan fokus pada energy transition, green technology, carbon capture, dan ESG integration. Ekonomi digital memerlukan pemahaman mendalam tentang solusi teknologi untuk tantangan iklim, kebijakan berkelanjutan, dan teknologi ramah lingkungan yang membentuk masa depan yang berkelanjutan.

## Learning Roadmap

### Phase 1: Sustainability Foundation (0-6 bulan)
1. **Climate Science**: Understanding climate change
2. **Environmental Policy**: Global agreements, regulations
3. **Renewable Energy**: Solar, wind, storage basics
4. **Sustainability Metrics**: ESG, carbon accounting
5. **Green Business**: Sustainable business models

### Phase 2: Technology Specialization (6-12 bulan)
1. **Clean Energy Systems**: Advanced renewable technologies
2. **Carbon Technologies**: Capture, storage, utilization
3. **Green Transportation**: EV, sustainable mobility
4. **Smart Cities**: Urban sustainability solutions
5. **Environmental Data**: Monitoring, analysis, reporting

### Phase 3: Implementation Focus (12-18 bulan)
1. **Project Management**: Green project development
2. **Investment Analysis**: Sustainable finance, ESG
3. **Corporate Sustainability**: Business transformation
4. **Policy Analysis**: Regulatory impact, compliance
5. **Innovation Strategy**: Clean tech development

### Phase 4: Expert Level (18+ bulan)
1. **Sustainability Leadership**: Corporate sustainability strategy
2. **Climate Finance**: Green bonds, climate investment
3. **Technology Transfer**: Clean tech commercialization
4. **Policy Development**: Climate policy design
5. **Global Impact**: International sustainability initiatives
